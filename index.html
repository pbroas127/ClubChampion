How to play

  1. Pick a formation. Every shape fields the same 7 players (1 GK + 6 outfield) — but a back three defends better while three up top outscores everyone. Balance is king.
  2. Spin the slot. Each round locks in a random club and an exact year (1990–2026). You might land on Barcelona 2011… or Man City's third-tier 1999.
  3. Draft a player from that club & year to fill an open position. You can't pick the same player twice.
  4. Use your skips wisely. One Year skip (same club, new year) and one Club skip (fresh club). A bad roll early is a hole you can't dig out of.
  5. Simulate the season. Your squad's five categories — Attack, Creativity, Defence, Physical, Goalkeeping — are summed and run through a non-linear engine. One weak area caps your whole season.
  6. Go unbeaten. Only a squad with no weak link can finish a 38-game season with 0 losses. Beat the CPU by out-drafting them, then winning the final.
