Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Club Champion ("we", "us", "the app") is a for-fun football squad-drafting game. This policy explains what information we collect when you use the app, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

Club Champion only collects what's needed to run your account and save your progress. We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or third-party data brokers.

CategoryWhatWhy
AccountEmail address, hashed password (or Google account identifier if you sign in with Google)To create and secure your account, sign you in, and let you reset your password
ProfileUsername you chooseDisplayed to other players (friends, ranked opponents, leaderboards)
Gameplay dataSeason results, match history, ranked rank/rating, win-loss record, drafted-player collection, streaksTo save your progress and show your stats, history, and collection
Social dataFriend requests/friendships, head-to-head records, game invites, and any player reports you file or are named inTo power the Friends and Ranked features, and to review reports of abusive behavior
Technical dataStandard server logs kept by our hosting and database providers (e.g. IP address, timestamps)Security, abuse prevention, and keeping the service running

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, camera data, health data, or payment information — the app has no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

2. How we store your data

Account and gameplay data is stored with our backend provider, Supabase, which hosts our database and handles authentication. Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS). We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties for advertising or marketing.

3. Third-party services

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and real-time features (acts as our data processor).
  • Google Sign-In — optional; if you choose to sign in with Google, Google shares your basic account identifier with us to authenticate you. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — hosts the app's static files and web traffic.

4. Your choices and rights

  • Access/Export — your stats, history, and collection are visible in-app under the Stats tab at any time.
  • Correction — you can change your username and password from Settings.
  • Deletion — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete my account. This immediately and irreversibly removes your account, profile, stats, friendships, and match history. This action cannot be undone.
  • Wipe stats only — Settings also lets you wipe just your saved stats while keeping your account.

5. Children's privacy

Club Champion is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we'll delete it.

6. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is deleted immediately and is not recoverable.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encrypted connections, database access controls, row-level security) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
